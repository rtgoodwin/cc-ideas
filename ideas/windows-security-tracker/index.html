Methodology

This tracker monitors publicly disclosed vulnerabilities (CVEs) affecting Windows 10 that were published after the end of support date (October 14, 2025). Data is updated daily from official sources. Severity ratings are based on CVSS v3.1 scores. "Actively Exploited" status is confirmed by CISA's Known Exploited Vulnerabilities catalog.
